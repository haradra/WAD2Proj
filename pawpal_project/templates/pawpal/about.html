About us

Have you ever had a bad day and wanted to find a furry creature that is always happy to cuddle? Do you find yourself lonely and need a bit of warmth? Or do you just want to spend some time actively with an energetic companion?

Regardless of your reason, now you have the opportunity to get all of that!

Our new web application PawPal is going to enable its users to connect with animal owners in their neighbourhood and arrange a meeting. By allowing the user to take an animal for a walk, feed it or pet it, and enabling them to “rent” an animal for some time, the new web application is meant to give the users the opportunity to unwind, reduce stress and make new connections while actively spending time with animals (and possibly their owners).

With the ability to rate an animal as easy/difficult to care and the ability to rate the animal as well as the user, the application will make it easy to find a match, that would suit their skills and needs, even for the inexperienced animal lovers.

The users will be able to contact each other once they’ve expressed the interest in the pet.
